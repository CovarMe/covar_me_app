Welcome to CovarMe

As part of the 2016/17 cohort of the Data Science course at the Barcelona Graduate School of Economics, we have build this application to implement some of the tools and techniques gained during the course and to showcase our skills in data-processing and computing.


This application stores daily stock returns for (very, very) many companies from 2010 onwards. You can select the stock tickers that you are interested in and build your own portfolio. Based on your portfolio selection, the application computes estimated risk versus return, relationship between your chosen stocks and interesting portfolio summary statistics.
